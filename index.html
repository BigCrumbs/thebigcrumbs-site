About

Big Crumbs originated from a single question: “What happens when we choose to pursue our interests as aggressively as possible, while framing every decision through the lens of originality?”

What began as a creative outlet and a sort of “developmental lab” has grown into something broader. Alongside the content, Big Crumbs focuses on projects rooted in the same belief: that the best things are made with genuine interest and a clear sense of purpose.
