Big Crumbs LLC is an independent mobile app studio focused on productivity and self-regulation software for iOS and Android.

About

Big Crumbs LLC is a Wyoming-based independent software company building mobile applications for iOS and Android. Our focus is on tools that give people more control over how they spend their attention.

Screen Tariff is our flagship app — a productivity tool that introduces a voluntary buffer period before accessing distracting apps, helping users build healthier habits around their most addictive apps.
